Lead Generation Executive (LGE)
We are seeking a talented sales professional with a successful track record for our Lead Generation Executive position. The ideal candidates will possess the ability to learn, to work in a fast paced environment, and to take pride in their work.
Roles And Responsibilities:
  • Analyze different markets to generate leads
  • Collect the leads from different campaigns
  • Generating and qualifying leads through cold calling
  • Coordinate with customer to explain about our products and services
  • Follow-up with clients through email or telephonic about new requirements or the expansion of the projects or the status update
  • Convert inquiries into sales by answering Outbound/Inbound calls
  • Maintain and update the lead generation database
  • Ensuring all the leads are well qualified by identifying the correct decision makers and understanding their requirements.
  • Build relationships with contacts for future opportunity development.
  • Familiarizing yourself with all products and services offered by our company.

Telemarketing Executive (TE)
We are seeking a talented sales professional with a successful track record for our Telemarketing Executive position. The ideal candidates will possess the ability to learn, to work in a fast paced environment, and to take pride in their work.
Roles And Responsibilities:
  • Contact individuals and businesses by telephone to promote products and services over a telephone.
  • Adjust a well-tailored sales pitch and deliver it efficiently to the customers also keeping in mind that they fulfill each and every requirements of a customer.
  • The Candidate must be good in explaining the features of the goods or products/services which he/she is trying to sell.
  • Should be very persuasive in order to convince the customers to buy the product by illustrating it in such a way that it meets their needs.
  • Keep a record of each and every personal detail of the prospect in their system and should also maintain all the conversations made over a telephone for future references.
  • Schedule appointments so that the sales team gets in touch with the probable customers.
  • Most importantly, a telemarketer needs to follow up with the prospect on what they had discussed in the last phone call.
  • Should be quite customer friendly and should be able to persuade the users.
  • Must understand what the prospect requires and accordingly act on implementing and provide a relevant solution to them in a short span of time.
  • The main objective of a telemarketer is to promote the growth of the business by expanding the company's customer network.
